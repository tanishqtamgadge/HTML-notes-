Welcome to Segmentic Tags!

Segmentic tags are a type of HTML markup language that allows you to divide your web pages into different sections or segments. They provide a clear structure and organization for your content, making it easier to maintain and update.

Some Common Segmentic Tags:

  • Header (h1-h6)
  • Navigation (nav)
  • Main content (main)
  • Article (article)
  • Section (section)
  • Aside (aside)
  • Footer (footer)
